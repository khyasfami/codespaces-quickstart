Strategy & Recommendations

System / Service Availability — Existing Strategy

1. IPBC is an absolutely critical business function, underscoring its importance to overall operations.
2. Any unavailability of the IPBC system will have a direct impact on unifi TV services.

System Recovery Plan — Existing Strategy

1. The IPTV system operates on a 70/30 capacity split across two locations: Cyberjaya and TTDI.
2. In the event of a disaster, both locations can function as Disaster Recovery (DR) sites, supporting minimum operational capabilities based on their respective setups.

System Recovery Plan — Additional Strategy

Tech refresh on Q4 2026 — Modernisation of IPBC platform to address aging/EOL hardware and software.

Network / Recovery Plan — Existing Strategy

In the event of a system failure, the vendor will replace the faulty unit with the same model or an equivalent alternative, ensuring no disruption to operations within the agreed vendor SLA.
